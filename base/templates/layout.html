About


With GenerationFlash, students can turn their lecture notes into dynamic Anki flashcards in seconds. Just upload your notes, and let GenerationFlash generate customized flashcards for effective, rapid learning and revision. Whether prepping for exams or reinforcing key concepts, GenerationFlash helps you retain information faster, empowering you to study smarter, not harder.
